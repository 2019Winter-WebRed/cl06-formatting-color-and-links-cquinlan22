About Field Hockey

Field hockey was first played in 19th Century Pakistan, it is a non-contact sport that consists of htting a small ball with a stick made of wood, carbon, or fiber glass. You scorre by passing the ball to teammates and getting the ball past the goalie into the goal. There are 11 players on the field at a time. Right now it is a very popular sport in The U.S and Canada, but throughout history it has been played in Egypt, New Zeland,Hong Kong, and Australlia.
